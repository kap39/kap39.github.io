STEM Ambassador

I have been a STEM Ambassador since 2017, joining the program in my third year of undergrad as an Ambassador to the Wales Hub. Since then I have volunteered as on-the-day support at large events such as Brain Games organised by the School of Psychology Cardiff University, and Maths Inspiration at the Bristol Hippodrome. I have also worked as part of a team running smaller workshops in primary schools teaching coding with the BBC Micro:bit, where students designed, created and coded a wearable product which played rock paper scissors upon shaking the device.
